Strip & Wax

Restore worn commercial VCT floors

Strip and wax service is built for commercial floors with old wax buildup, dull appearance, heavy traffic wear, or finish that no longer protects the floor properly.

The process removes old wax buildup, deep cleans the floor surface, applies new wax, and restores a cleaner shine with renewed protection. It is ideal for worn commercial VCT floors that need a full reset.

  • Old wax removal
  • Deep cleaning process
  • New wax application
  • Shine and protection

Floor Scrubbing

Deep cleaning without full stripping

Floor scrubbing is a practical option when a commercial floor needs deeper cleaning, but the existing finish does not need to be fully removed.

This service helps remove dirt and buildup, maintain floor appearance, and extend the useful life of the wax before a larger restoration service is needed.

  • Removes dirt buildup
  • Refreshes appearance
  • Supports wax life
  • Good mid-cycle service

Floor Buffing

Routine shine restoration

Floor buffing helps restore shine, reduce visible scuffs, and keep commercial floors looking better between deeper service visits.
